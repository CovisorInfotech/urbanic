RETURN & REFUND

We have an easy return policy and accept returns within 10 days from the date of delivery for most of the items. There are certain items that cannot be returned: innerwear, lingerie & sleepwear, swimwear, jewellery. Please note, the amount will be refunded for the items only, other fees like shipping fee, COD service charges etc. are non-refundable. For more info on return, please check our Return Policy.

Please follow the simple steps mentioned below to submit your return request within 10 days from the order is delivered –
1. Login to your account on Urbanic app from which you have placed the order
2. Go to "Account" section from the home page
3. From the order list, select the order you want to apply for return
4. In the return application, select the products and quantity you want to return. In case of multiple products, please apply for return for each item by clicking on the return option against the item.
5. Select reason for return and write some details about the issue you have faced
6. Upload images for our team to review your request
7. Provide required details like pickup address, expected time for pickup and bank details in case of COD order
8. Submit the request.

We are sorry if you have received the wrong amount for your refund. For such instances, please contact our customer support team either via Chat or email us at support@urbanic.com with the complete details of your order and refund amount. We will help to resolve the issue within 24-48 hours.

You may have not received the refund due to the following reasons -
1. You must have not shared the bank account details with us. To share the bank account details, please contact our customer support team either through Chat or email us at support@urbanic.com.
2. The bank account details shared by you were incorrect/invalid. Hence, the refund was not processed. We would request you to submit the bank details again by following the below steps –
1. Log into the Urbanic Msite (Mobile Browser). Go to the "Account" option [ Bottom right Corner]
2. Click on the Option "Return and Refund".
3. Select the Order for which the refund has failed.
4. Fill the Bank details correctly.
5. Re check the bank details and click on " Confirm to Submit". The refund will be Immediately processed.
